Leadership and Development

Catholic Youth Community (CYC)

President (AY23/24)

As a leader in the CYC, I help to plan sessions and fellowships for our fellow members in community. We leaders also watch out for each other and our community members especially when we are struggling or need help. CYC is a place for us to embrace our Catholic faith and to walk alongside one another as we grow in faith.

InfoSec Club

SubCommittee Member (AY23/24, AY24/25)

In InfoSec Club, we work together to plan for events such as the Youth Cyber Exploration Programme and Capture the Flag hackathons. As a SubComm member, I help out in the ideation and planning process, using my technical skills to create guides or presentations for students, and to create challenges that will test the abilities of those who participate in our hackathons.

National Civil Defence Cadet Corps (NCDCC)

Company Sergeant Major (CSM, 2020-2021)

I led the Montfort NCDCC unit as the CSM and worked together with my squadmates to plan trainings and mentor the cadets. As the CSM, I was the liaison for the Teacher Officers and the Cadet Lieutenants, and worked with them to strengthen our unit. I received the SCDF-NCDCC Pinnacle Award and the Unit Best Cadet Award for my hard work and efforts at reshaping our unit.

Montfort Student Council

Senior Councillor (2020-2021)

Working together with the Student Council EXCOs, I played a pivotal role in planning many school events such as, Sec 1 Orientation in 2020 and 2021, and the Student Leaders' Investiture in 2021. I represented Montfort Secondary in many events such as in our Secondary 1 outreach to primary schools, the Temasek Leadership Showdown in 2019, as well as in the Brunei-Singapore Twinning Programme in 2019.
